Rust what?

Something new! RustNL has organized plenty of Rust meetups, with usually more people wanting to join than we had places available. At RustNL 2023 we welcome over 250 Rustaceans for a full day of Rust!

  • One day on-site event, 9-18h
  • One track ~ 8 talks
  • Lunch and closing drinks included
  • Interaction space and plenty of breaks to talk
  • Paid tickets for companies and individuals
  • Low-priced tickets available for students
  • Held close to Amsterdam central station

Please note: although we're called "RustNL" everyone is welcome to join us in Amsterdam, not just Dutchies.

Bringing together the Rust community

Not only the Dutch Rust community will gather during RustNL 2023. Rust open-source developers brought together by Futurewei will join in and work together on the future of UI frameworks in the Rust ecosystem during the week of the event.

That group includes members of the Rust project teams (Library, Lang, Compiler, Dev tools etc) who will also be around and join the event.

Getting there

The event will take place at OBA Oosterdok, 6th & 7th floor:
Oosterdokskade 143
1011 DK Amsterdam

Directions
The OBA venue on the Oosterdokskade is easily accessible by public transport. Take the metro, tram, bus, ferry or train to Amsterdam Central Station. From there it is a 5 to 10 minute walk east across Oosterdokseiland.

For cyclists there is an underground, guarded bicycle shed next to the OBA. Your bike is safe and dry there for free.

Are you coming by car? Parking is possible in Parking Centrum Oosterdok, the cheapest parking garage in the city center. This parking garage is easily accessible from the A10 ring road. It is only a few minutes walk from the garage.

The OBA building is a public library. The event venue is located on the 6th and 7th floor. Take the escalator or elevator to get to the 6th.

Questions? Contact us via 2023@rustnl.org.

About RustNL

Stichting Rust Nederland, RustNL for short, a non-profit that strives to promote the implementation of the Rust programming language in the Netherlands and Europe.

We organize meetups and events in The Netherlands to bring the Rust community together.
